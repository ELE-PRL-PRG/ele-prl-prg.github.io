Mission

A mission of the ELE project is to look at the entire language stack, from the semantics all the way down to the details of its realization in machine code, as well as on how the language is used from libraries all the way up to pseudo code hosted on discussion forums. It is our strongest belief that only when weighing the costs and tradeoffs across the entire language stack that we will be able to come up with solutions that can truly improve the state of the art.
